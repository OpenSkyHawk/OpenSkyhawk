# Center Console

The center console sits between the pilot's knees in the A-4E — it carries the armament
management controls and the central flight instruments. It's the most developed part of the
cockpit: the **Armament Group** is the only panel group in the whole build with real hardware
designed.

## Panel groups

| Panel Group | Status | NODE_ID |
|-------------|--------|---------|
| [Armament Group](armament/index.md) | Hardware designed, firmware Phase 6 | 1 |
| Flight Instruments | Planned | TBD |

## Armament Group

The active work. Host board plus two breakout boards have completed schematics; firmware is
a Phase 6 stub pending end-to-end integration. See the
[Armament Group overview](armament/index.md) for the per-panel hardware status.

## Flight Instruments

!!! note "Planned — contributions welcome"
    The center console's flight instruments — the Wheels & Flaps position indicator, the
    Caution Panel ladder lights, the Pilot Advisory ladder lights, and the AN/APG-53A Radar
    Indicator — are **planned**. No hardware is designed yet. Want to help build one? See
    [How to Contribute](../../contributing/index.md).
