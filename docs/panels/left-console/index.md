# Left Console

The left console in the A-4E carries navigation and communications gear, plus the cockpit
lighting controls — the throttle quadrant, the radios, TACAN, compass, and the
interior/exterior light panels.

## Planned panel groups

- **Throttle Panel** — the throttle quadrant (switches and indicators); routes through
  DCS-BIOS. Note: the throttle *axis* is separate — it's a HID axis on the replica throttle,
  see [Flight Controls](../../flight-controls/index.md).
- **Navigation & Comms** — AN/APG-53A Radar Control, AN/ARC-51A UHF Radio, AN/ARN-52 TACAN,
  Compass Control
- **Lighting** — Interior Lights, Exterior Lights

!!! note "All planned — no hardware yet"
    Nothing on the left console is designed yet — no schematics, no PCBs, no firmware. These
    are scoped panel groups waiting for someone to start them.

## Want to help design a left console panel?

This is open ground — a good place to take ownership of a panel group from scratch. The
[Armament Group](../center-console/armament/index.md) is the worked example to follow for
